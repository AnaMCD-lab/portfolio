Bread Recipe

For two loaves, you will need:

  • 100 g Mature, active levain
  • 400 g White Flour
  • 100 g Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1 3/4 cups Water

Procedure:

Mix all the ingredients with your hands. Let it rest at room temperature.
